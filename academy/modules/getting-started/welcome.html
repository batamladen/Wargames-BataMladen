Welcome to WBM!

Welcome to our cybersecurity training & learning platform!
Whether you're new to cybersecurity or looking to sharpen your skills, you've come to the right place.

What are Wargames?

Wargames are a hands-on cybersecurity training platform where you can:

  • Learn through practical challenges
  • Develop real-world security skills
  • Compete with other participants
  • Track your progress and achievements

How to start the WBM Challenges?

To start playing the wargames:

  1. Select a challenge you like in
  2. Press the start button on level 0
  3. Copy the ssh command in your terminal
  4. Enter the password for level 0 (provided in the challenge page)
  5. Find the flag - Submit it - Start the next level with the flag you found as the password for the next level.

How to start with the WBM Academy

The academy section is community driven, with lessons written by professionals at the field.

To start learning, simply select a path or module from the academy section and follow the modules and lessons step-by-step. Each lesson will guide you through different topics, providing explanations, examples, and tasks to complete. As you progress, you'll earn "Aura Points" which reflect your learning achievements on the platform.

You can track your progress through the dashboards provided in the main academy page and in each module.

Once you finish a modue you can always come back to review it again.
