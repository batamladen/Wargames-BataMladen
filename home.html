Welcome

This platform hosts a series of cybersecurity challenges designed to test your red and blue team skills.
Each challenge consists of multiple levels that differ in difficulty and bring you different amount of aura points.

Once you complete all levels in a challenge, you will recive a certificate of completion for that challenge on your mail whitin 7 days.


How It Works

To participate in the challenges, you'll need to SSH into our systems:

  • Each level has its own dedicated port on our server
  • The Entry Point password is always provided for the first level
  • Subsequent levels require you to find flags that serve as passwords
  • Each level's flag is the password for the next level

Example SSH

ssh username@123.456.654.321 -p 1234

IMPORTANT: Take notes of all passwords/flags you find!

Ready to test your skills? Click on a challenge you like and start conquering.

Rules

  • Please no DoS attacks of any kind
  • Don't bruteforce flag submission
  • Kindly report any bugs you find
  • Have Fun!
