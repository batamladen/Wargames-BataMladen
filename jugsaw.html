Jugsaw

“Live or die. Make your choice.”

Welcome to Jugsaw, a psychological reverse engineering challenge inspired by the Saw film series.
Each level in this wargame challenge mirrors a scene from a corresponding Saw movie.
Players must confront traps, cryptic binaries, and moral dilemmas coded deep into each level.

The mysterious figure known as Jugsaw appears through a flickering screen to deliver your objective... then vanishes.
Just like in the movies, there are no coincidences — only consequences.

You'll need critical thinking, time management, and the willingness to truly understand the system you're trapped in.
Reverse binaries, bypass traps, decode obfuscated clues — all while a timer ticks in the background.

There’s no hand-holding.
There’s no escape without sacrifice.
The only question is:

How far are you willing to go to survive the game?
